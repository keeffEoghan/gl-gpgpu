Module [main]

GPGPU state-stepping - declaratively maps optimal draw passes, shaders, WebGL resources, inputs, outputs - lets you focus on your logic, BYORenderer.

Decouples logic from rendering approach/engine. The modules and many hooks may be used as given, or piecemeal, or overridden.
