Module: gl-gpgpu

GPGPU state-stepping - maps optimal draw passes, shaders, GL resources, inputs, outputs; lets you focus on your logic - BYORenderer. Decouples logic from rendering approach/engine. The modules and many hooks may be used as given, or piecemeal, or overridden.

(static) gpgpu(api, stateopt, toopt) → {object}
